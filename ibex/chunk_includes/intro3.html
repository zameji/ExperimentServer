Thank you for your responses! Now for the fun part.

In this experiment, you will be reading English sentences one word at a time.

On the next screen, you will see a series of lines. Press the space bar to make the first word show up.

After the first word shows up, press the space bar to see the next word.

Continue pressing the space bar after each word.

The sentences are not related to each other. They do not form a story or have a set order.

Please try to read as normally as possible. There are about 300 sentences, so we expect it will take about an hour. If you need to take a brief break (to drink some water, go to the bathroom, etc.) please do so while you're on a question screen, not in the middle of a sentence! Halfway through the experiment, you'll have the chance to take a longer break.
