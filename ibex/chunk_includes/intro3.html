You will now start the longest part of this experiment, in which you will be reading English sentences one word at a time.

On the next screen, you will see a series of lines. Press the space bar to make the first word show up.

After the first word shows up, press the space bar to see the next word.

Press the space bar after each following word.

The sentences are not related to each other. They do not form a story or have a set order.

Some sentences are followed by a question. Select the BEST answer of the options provided. You will be told if you select the wrong answer. If you select the correct answer, you will continue to the next sentence with no message.

Please try to read as normally as possible. We expect this part will take about 45 minutes. If you need to take a brief break (to drink some water, go to the bathroom, etc.) please do so while you're on a question screen, not in the middle of a sentence! When you are done with this section, you'll have the chance to take a longer break.

It is important that your computer remains plugged in for the entire experiment and that you close all background programs (Skype, games, email, etc.) You also need a steady internet connection for the duration of the experiment.

If you lose connection, shut the browser, or lose power, you will unfortunately not be able to continue the experiment. Please contact us if this happens.
