COOKIE POLICY

Cookies are small text files that a website stores on your computer. On this website, we utilize cookies to track your progess through the site.

This way, the site knows which section of the experiment you should be on. If you lose your place, the website can bring you to the next incomplete page.

These cookies are strictly necessary for the website -- without them, the website will not know which part to take you to, and will crash.

Our cookies are stored for less than 3 days, then they will be automatically deleted.

We do not gather any personal information in the cookies or share them or any of your data with any third parties.


However, you do not have to agree to the use of our cookies. If you do not agree, you cannot use the website.

If you do not want to use cookies, do not proceed past the first page.
You can also delete your cookies if you have already proceeded past the first page. This will also make the correct use of the site impossible for you.

Please contact us if you would like more information.
