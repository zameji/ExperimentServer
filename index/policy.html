Cookies

What are cookies

Cookies are text files that contain small pieces of data. They are saved in your browser and allow some of the modern functions of websites. For example, cookies allow websites to recognize that you have visited them before or keep track of which parts of a website you have visited.

How do we use cookies

The experiment differs between individual participants. We use some cookies to personalize the experiment and combine your answers in the individual parts of the experiment. The cookies that we use are not used to track you around the Internet, but only to make this experiment work. We do not use any other cookies than those that we need. We do not store any cookies in your browser before you allow us to do that.

How to opt out of cookies for this website.

If you do not want us to use cookies, you can tell us that in the introductory questionaire at the start of the experiment. Do note that in such cases, the functionality may be limited.
