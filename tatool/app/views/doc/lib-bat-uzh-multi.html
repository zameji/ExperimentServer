Task Batteries

Multifacet Working Memory Testing and Training Package (UZH-Multi)

This task battery comprises two versions of the Tower of Fame task (Complex Span and Switching tasks coming soon), one for testing baseline performance and one for training.

Background

The tasks included in this package were used in a study investigating the effects of working memory training in younger and older adults. The tasks were designed based on the facet model of working memory capacity by Oberauer et al. (2008) and originally programmed in Java-based Tatool.

Modules

  • Testing (Tower of Fame task; Complex Span and Switching tasks coming soon)
  • Training (Tower of Fame task; Complex Span and Switching tasks coming soon)

How to Cite

von Bastian, C. C., Langer, N., Jäncke, L., & Oberauer, K. (2013). Effects of working memory training in young and old adults. Memory & Cognition, 41(4), 611-624. doi: 10.3758/s13421-012-0280-7
