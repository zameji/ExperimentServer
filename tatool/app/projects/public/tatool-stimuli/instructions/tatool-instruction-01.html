Add a Module


This Module will give you a quick introduction to the features available in your Modules screen.

You're currently running the Instruction Module which means you've already added a Module from the list of publically available Modules, by clicking the corresponding add button. After clicking the button, the Module is added to your list of installed Modules (My Modules) and removed from the list of Public Modules.


You can continue the introduction by pressing the right arrow key on your keyboard or by clicking on the right arrow button with your mouse.
