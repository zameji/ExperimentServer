How good are you at switching between two tasks?

You'll see digits between 1 and 9. You have to classify these digits according to their parity (odd/even) or their magnitude (less/greater than 5). The difficulty lies in only one of the two dimensions being relevant at a time. Which one that is can randomly switch. Thus, take care whether you're currently being asked for the parity or magnitude when responding. A cue at the top of the screen shows you which dimension is the relevant one:
