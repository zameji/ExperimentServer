Instructions

In sum, you are asked to do the same for both squares and letters.

If you need to do a 1-back, it is 1-back for squares and letters. If you need to do a 2-back, it is 2-back for squares and letters, etc.

Whenever a new pair of square and letter is presented, you will have to do one of the following:

  • you have to push the LEFT ARROW for a matching square
  • you have to push the RIGHT ARROW for a matching letter
  • you have to push the DOWN ARROW for a square AND letter match
  • you do not have to push a key
