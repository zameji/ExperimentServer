Instructions

Now we will try selecting the colored arrow key that matches the color of the word, not the meaning of the word.
In this practice round, you will see words for which you have to decide as quickly and as accurately as possible whether their color corresponds to the left, up, down, or right arrow key. Indicate your response by pressing the corresponding key as explained below.



  • Press the left arrow key if the color of the word is red.
  • Press the up arrow key if the color of the word is yellow.
  • Press the down arrowkey if the color of the word is green.
  • Press the right arrow key if the color of the word is blue.
