Instruction

How good are you at remembering associations between colors and shapes?

In this task you have to memorize shapes in different colors and update these color-shape associations correspondingly whenever new information is presented.

For this purpose, in the memorization phase, five shapes (circle, triangle, square, diamond, and hexagon) are displayed in different colors. You have to memorize these color-shape associations.

Important: In some trials, you'll be asked to recall the correct associations immediately after the memorization phase. Therefore, make sure to memorize them really well.
