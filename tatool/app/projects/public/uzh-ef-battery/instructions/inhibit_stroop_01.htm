Instruction

This task measures the ability to focus on relevant and to ignore irrelevant information.

You'll have to count the number of characters presented and press the corresponding number key (on the top row of your keyboard). As soon as you pressed a key, the next string of characters will be presented. The difficulty is that the number of characters being displayed can differ from their actual magnitude.

Please try to answer as accurately and as quickly as possible.
