Instruction

This task measures the ability to focus on relevant and to ignore irrelevant information.

You'll have to decide whether the centrally presented letter in a string of characters is a vowel (A or E) or a consonant (S or T). React only to the letter in the center and try to ignore the surrounding characters.


Press the left arrow key if the letter is a vowel.

Press the right arrow key if the letter is a consonant.

Please try to answer as accurately and as quickly as possible.
