Instruction

In the following task, numbers are sequentially presented that you have to memorize. The numbers are interleaved by another task, in which you have to decide as quickly and as accurately as possible whether a digit is smaller than 5 (e.g., '2') or greater than 5 (e.g., '7'). Indicate your response by pressing the correspondig key:


  • Press the left arrow key if the digit is smaller than 5.
  • Press the right arrow key if the digit is greater than 5.

After 3 to 5 numbers interleaved by the other task, you'll be asked to recall the numbers you memorized in the beginning. Please type one number at a time in the text field, and confirm after each number by pressing Enter.

Start the task by pressing the right arrow key on your keyboard.
You can exit the task at any time by pressing the escape key.
