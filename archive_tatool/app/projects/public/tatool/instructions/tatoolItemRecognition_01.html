Instruction

How good are you at remembering pictures?

You'll see a series of pictures that you'll have to study. Afterwards, you'll be shown another series of pictures, for each of which you'll have to decide as quickly and as accurately as possible whether it was presented during the study phase or whether it's a new picture. A cue will signal the start of the recognition phase.

During the recognition phase, please indicate your response by pressing the corresponding key.


  • Press the left arrow key if the picture is old (i.e. has been shown during the study phase).
  • Press the right arrow key if the picture is new (i.e. has not been shown during the study phase).


Please press the right arrow key to proceed to the next page.
