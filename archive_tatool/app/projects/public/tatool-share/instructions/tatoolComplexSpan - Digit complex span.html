Instructions

In this task, numbers are shown that you have to memorise. For example, 53.

After each number, you will be shown a digit in blue. For example, 3. When you see the blue number you have to decide as quickly and accurately as possible if the digit is smaller than 5 or greater than 5.

Indicate your answer by pressing the corresponding key:

  • Press the left arrow key if the digit is smaller than 5.
  • Press the right arrow key if the digit is greater than 5.

After 2 to 5 numbers and digits, you will be asked to type in the numbers you had to memorise in order. Please type one number at a time in the text box and push Enter after each number.
We are first going to do an example.



Start the task by pressing the right arrow key on your keyboard.
You can exit the task at any time by pressing the escape key.
