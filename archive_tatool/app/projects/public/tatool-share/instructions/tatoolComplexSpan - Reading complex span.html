Instructions

In the following task, nouns are shown that you have to read out loud and memorise. For example, chair.

After each noun, you will be shown a sentence. Read each sentence out loud as well. When you see the sentence you have to decide as quickly and accurately as possible if the sentence makes sense.

Indicate your answer by pressing the corresponding key:

  • Press the left arrow key if the sentence does not make sense.
  • Press the right arrow key if the sentence does make sense.

After 2 to 5 nouns and sentences, you will be asked to type in the nouns you had to memorise in order. Please type one word at a time in the text box and push Enter after each word.
We are first going to do an example.



Start the task by pressing the right arrow key on your keyboard.
You can exit the task at any time by pressing the escape key.
