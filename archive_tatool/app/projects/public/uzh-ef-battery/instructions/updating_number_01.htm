Instruction

How good are you at remembering associations between colors and digits?

In this task you have to memorize colors of digits with different values and update these color-digit associations correspondingly whenever new information is presented.

For this purpose, in the memorization phase, four digits (between 1 and 9) are displayed in different colors (red, blue, green, and orange). You have to memorize these color-digit associations.

Important: In some trials, you'll be asked to recall the correct associations immediately after the memorization phase. Therefore, make sure to memorize them really well.
