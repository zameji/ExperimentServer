Instruction

This task measures the ability to focus on relevant and to ignore irrelevant information.

You'll have to decide whether a circle is green or red by pressing a left or a right key. The difficulty lies in the circle being presented in the left or right half of the screen independent of its color. Thus, the circle's position won't always correspond to the location of the correct answer key (i.e., left or right).



Press the left arrow key if the circle is green.


Press the right arrow key if the circle is red.

Please try to answer as accurately and as quickly as possible.


Please press the right arrow key to proceed to the next page.
