How good are you at switching between two tasks?

You'll see images of animals and objects that are of a different size in the real-world. You have to classify these images according to their animacy (animal/object) and their size (smaller/larger than a soccer ball). The difficulty lies in only one of the two dimensions being relevant at a time. Which one that is can randomly switch. Thus, take care whether you're currently being asked for the animacy or size when responding. A cue at the top of the screen shows you which dimension is the relevant one:


Animacy


Does the image show an animal or an object?		 Size


Is the animal or object smaller or larger than a soccer ball?
