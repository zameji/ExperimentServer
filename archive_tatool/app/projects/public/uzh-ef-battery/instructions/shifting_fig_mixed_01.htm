How good are you at switching between two tasks?

You'll see objects with different shapes and colors. You have to classify these objects according to their shape (round/angular) and their color (blue/green). The difficulty lies in only one of the two dimensions being relevant at a time. Which one that is can randomly switch. Thus, take care whether you're currently being asked for the shape or color when responding. A cue at the top of the screen shows you which dimension is the relevant one:
