How good are you at remembering associations between letters and their positions?

In this task you have to memorize consonants at different positions and update these consonant-position associations correspondingly whenever new information is presented.

Important: In some trials, you'll be asked to recall the correct associations immediately after the memorization phase. Therefore, make sure to memorize them really well.
