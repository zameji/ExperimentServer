Instruction

After all animals have been shown, the grid will be cleared and all animal pictures or words that appeared before will be available below the grid.
It's your task now to drag and drop the animals to the positions where they appeared before. Think before you move, as you can't change the position of an animal once you've put it into a position. As soon as you've moved the last animal to a position, you'll receive a visual feedback indicating which animals were placed correctly (green) and which animals were placed wrongly (red).



Start the task by pressing the right arrow key on your keyboard.
