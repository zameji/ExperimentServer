Instruction

After all digits have been shown, the grid will be cleared and all digit words or numbers that appeared before will be available below the grid.
It's your task now to drag and drop the digits to the positions where they appeared before. Think before you move, as you can't change the position of a digit once you've put it into a position. As soon as you've moved the last digit to a position, you'll receive a visual feedback indicating which digits were placed correctly (green) and which digits were placed wrongly (red).



Start the task by pressing the right arrow key on your keyboard.
