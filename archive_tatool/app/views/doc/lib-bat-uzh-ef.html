Task Batteries

Executive Functions (UZH-EF)

This task battery comprises three tasks for each of the executive functions inhibition, shifting, and updating.

  • Background
  • Modules
  • How to Cite

Background

This task battery was part of a larger cognitive assessment battery used in a study investigating the impact of bilingualism on cognitive ability. The tasks were modelled after the work by Miyake et al. (2000) and originally programmed in Java-based Tatool.

Modules

  • Inhibition (Simon, Flanker, and Stroop task)
  • Shifting (Shape/Color, Animacy/Size, and Parity/Magnitude Switching task)
  • Updating (Color, Letter, and Number Keep-Track task)

How to Cite
