How to cite

Please refer to this version of Tatool as Tatool Web and cite the following paper that describes the original version and general vision of Tatool:

von Bastian, C. C., Locher, A., & Ruflin, M. (2013). Tatool: A Java-Based Open-Source Programming Framework for Psychological Studies. Behavior Research Methods, 45(1), 108-115. doi: 10.3758/s13428-012-0224-y

Citations spread the word about Tatool amongst researchers, leading to a growing community and thereby more users who can support each other. In turn, a strong user base helps us to justify the continuous development of Tatool.
